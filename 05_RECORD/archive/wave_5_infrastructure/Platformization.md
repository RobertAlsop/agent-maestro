# Platformization

## Purpose

The process and architecture for turning Agent Maestro infrastructure into deployable platforms — orchestration platforms, enterprise AI operating systems, and vertical AI infrastructure.

## Purpose

Corresponds to Stage 5 of the [[Agent Maestro — Strategy]]. Platformization is the outcome of mature, repeatable infrastructure.

## Responsibilities

- Define platform packaging and deployment models
- Create multi-tenant governance isolation
- Enable platform-level authority management

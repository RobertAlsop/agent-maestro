# Wave 5.2 — Capture Seed

## Purpose

This seed formally closes the Wave 5.1 loop (Reflect → Capture) and opens Wave 5.2. It routes the output of the design phase into the next build phase.

## What Arrived

Wave 5.1 was a collaborative human-AI design session that produced a complete architectural redesign of Agent Maestro. The full account lives in [[Wave 5.1 — Design Session Retrospective]]. In brief: the vault was simplified from ~284 aspirational files to a clean 6-folder pipeline, authority packs (130+ files) were replaced by 2 role definitions, schema was reduced from 30 fields to 9, and the [[AM v1.0.0 — Design Blueprint]] was written as the build contract.

The retrospective identified that the vault now has good structure but unfinished joints — governance documents haven't been reviewed for post-restructure coherence, 39 concept files haven't been audited against Law 3, no roadmap or SOPs exist, and the pipeline itself isn't flowing (06_REFLECT was empty, 01_CAPTURE had no seeds).

## What Comes Next

The retrospective's brainstorm section (Part 3) contains the full set of future possibilities, from immediate build tasks through long-term self-improvement capabilities. These have been distilled into an approved proposal: [[Proposal — Wave 5.2 Pipeline Hardening]].

Wave 5.2 hardens the pipeline through six phases: close the 5.1 loop, review all governance documents collaboratively, walk every pipeline folder applying Law 3, consolidate the roadmap, document SOPs and workflows, and write the retrospective. Every phase is a human-AI working conversation, not an autonomous run.

## Routing

Triaged and routed. The approved proposal is in `03_DECIDE/proposals/`. Execution begins immediately.

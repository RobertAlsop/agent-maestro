# Operational Governance

## Purpose

The domain of governance as it operates day-to-day in running systems — as distinct from governance design or policy definition. Operational Governance covers runtime enforcement, evidence collection, escalation management, and the ongoing maintenance of governance integrity in live agent deployments.

## Context

Governance is not just a design-time activity. In live agent systems, governance must be continuously enforced, monitored, and maintained. This domain focuses on the operational side: how [[Compiled Governance Artifact|compiled governance artifacts]] are loaded and enforced, how escalations are handled, and how evidence is captured for auditability.

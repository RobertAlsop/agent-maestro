# Workflow Intelligence

## Definition

The application of agent reasoning to operational workflows, enabling adaptive, context-aware execution of organizational processes.

## Purpose

Workflow intelligence is the practical expression of [[Operational Intelligence]] inside specific business processes.

## Key Components

- Workflow mapping
- Context-sensitive decision points
- Agent execution steps
- [[Operational Outcome]] measurement

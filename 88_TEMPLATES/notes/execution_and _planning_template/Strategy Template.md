# Purpose
Define how an objective will be pursued over time.

# Context
Strategy explains why a chosen direction makes sense in the current conditions.

# Relationships
Explain how this strategy connects to the north star, governance, systems, or roadmap.

# Strategic Direction

## Objective
The outcome the strategy is designed to achieve.

## Approach
The guiding method or plan of action.

## Tradeoffs
What is being prioritized and what is being deprioritized.

## Success Metrics
Observable indicators that show whether the strategy is working.

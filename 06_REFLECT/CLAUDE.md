# 06_REFLECT — Reflect Stage

## Pipeline Position

Stage 6 of 6. Receives records from `05_RECORD/`. Closes the loop — outputs feed back into `01_CAPTURE/` as capture seeds for future work.

## Purpose

Look back and look forward. What happened? What did we learn? What comes next? This is the feedback stage of the irreducible loop — without it, the system cannot improve.

## Contents

Reflections live directly in this folder — one per wave, flat structure, no subfolders. Written using `Template — Reflection` from `02_KNOWLEDGE/templates/`. Each covers what happened, what we learned, what comes next, and process reflection.

Every wave MUST end with a reflection. The reflection is then distilled into a capture seed filed in `01_CAPTURE/` — this is how the loop closes.

Note: Brainstorms and forward-looking ideas are raw input — they belong in `01_CAPTURE/`, not here. Reflect looks back on completed work. The reflection's "What Comes Next" section is the bridge forward.

## Gate: Entry to Reflect

A wave or significant phase must be complete. Reflection without something to reflect on is premature.

## Gate: Exit to Capture

The reflection produces a capture seed. The seed references the reflection (wiki-link, not content duplication). The next wave begins from that seed.

## The Loop

```
06_REFLECT → capture seed → 01_CAPTURE → next wave
```

This is the fractal heartbeat. The same pattern at every scale.

# Human-Agent Collaboration

## Definition

The operational model in which humans and agents share responsibility for outcomes, with clearly defined handoff and escalation points.

## Purpose

Effective collaboration requires explicit authority boundaries. Without them, humans either over-trust agents or duplicate their work.

## Key Components

- Escalation triggers
- Human review gates
- Shared audit trails
- Trust calibration over time

# Governance Systems

## Definition

The policies, structures, and enforcement mechanisms that ensure agent systems operate within acceptable boundaries.

## Purpose

Governance systems are the organizational expression of Authority Architecture]]. They translate technical authority controls into organizational policy.

## Key Components

- Policy definition
- Authority pack deployment
- Audit and compliance
- Escalation and review processes

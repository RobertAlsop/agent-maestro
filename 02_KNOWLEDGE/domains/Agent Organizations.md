# Agent Organizations

## Purpose

The domain concerned with how organisations are designed, structured, and operated when agents are first-class participants — alongside humans — in organisational workflows, decisions, and delivery.

## Context

Agent Organizations are not just companies that use AI tools. They are organisations in which agents have defined roles, authority, and accountability — making them genuine organisational participants, not just software. This domain covers org design, role definition, authority architecture, and governance for agent-inclusive organisations.

# AI Executive Systems

## Purpose

The domain of AI systems operating at executive or strategic decision levels — systems where AI agents make or support high-consequence organisational decisions, set direction, and allocate resources within governed authority boundaries.

## Context

AI Executive Systems represent the frontier of AI-native organisations: deploying [[Executive Agent|Executive Agents]] that can autonomously manage complex operational domains, coordinate multi-agent teams, and make bounded strategic decisions — while remaining accountable to human governance structures.

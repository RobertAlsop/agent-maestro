# Controlled Evolution Pattern

## Definition

The pattern of evolving agent systems through graduated stages — experiment, validate, harden, scale — never skipping steps.

## Purpose

Uncontrolled growth produces brittle systems. Controlled evolution is Principle 11 of [[Agent Maestro — Principles]] and maps directly to the [[Agent Maestro — Strategy]] five-stage model.

## Key Components

1. Experiment at small scope
2. Validate outcomes against expected behaviour
3. Harden authority and governance
4. Scale with confidence

# Operational Intelligence

## Purpose

Define what Agent Maestro means by "operational intelligence" — the core domain concept that the entire system serves.

## Definition

Operational intelligence is the capability of a system to perceive its environment, interpret what it finds, make governed decisions, act on them, record the outcomes, and learn from the results — reliably, accountably, and at scale.

It is not artificial general intelligence. It is not model capability. It is the *operational layer* that sits between raw AI capability and real-world usefulness — the governance, workflows, memory, and feedback loops that make AI systems trustworthy enough to deploy in complex human contexts.

## What Makes It "Operational"

The word "operational" distinguishes this from theoretical AI research. Operational intelligence is:

- **Deployed** — it runs in real environments, not just labs
- **Governed** — every action passes through authority checks
- **Accountable** — every outcome is traceable to the decision that produced it
- **Evolving** — it improves through structured feedback, not uncontrolled mutation

## What Makes It "Intelligence"

The word "intelligence" means the system does more than execute scripts. It:

- **Perceives** — captures input from its environment (Capture stage)
- **Interprets** — classifies and connects information (Knowledge stage)
- **Decides** — evaluates options and commits to action (Decide stage)
- **Acts** — executes with skill and within authority (Execute stage)
- **Remembers** — records outcomes for future reference (Record stage)
- **Learns** — reflects on results and proposes improvements (Reflect stage)

This is the Irreducible Loop from [[Agent Maestro — Foundations]], applied as a capability rather than a process.

## The Governance Bottleneck

The [[Agent Maestro — North Star]] identifies the core problem AM solves: AI capability is advancing faster than the governance systems needed to deploy it safely. Operational intelligence is the answer — not smarter AI, but AI that works within structures that make it reliable and accountable.
